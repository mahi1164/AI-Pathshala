Our Story

AI Pathshala Jashpur was founded with the vision to bridge the digital divide and prepare the youth of Jashpur for a tech-driven future. We are committed to fostering a culture of innovation through accessible education and skill development in AI and related fields.

Our Mission

To empower the students, teachers, and youth of Jashpur district with cutting-edge knowledge in Artificial Intelligence, Prompting, ChatGPT, and emerging technologies, fostering a culture of innovation and digital literacy. AI Pathshala Jashpur aims to bridge the technological divide in rural areas, enhance employability, and create a sustainable ecosystem of learning and skill development. By providing accessible, hands-on training and resources, the initiative strives to nurture future-ready individuals who can contribute meaningfully to the nation’s progress.

Our Values

To establish Jashpur as a hub of innovation and digital excellence by nurturing a generation of technologically skilled individuals proficient in Artificial Intelligence, Prompting, ChatGpt, and emerging technologies. The vision is to create an inclusive and forward-thinking community where students, educators, and professionals collaborate to solve real-world problems, drive socioeconomic growth, and position Jashpur as a leader in the global AI revolution.
