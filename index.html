About Us

Our Story

AI Pathshala Jashpur was founded with the vision to bridge the digital divide and prepare the youth of Jashpur for a tech-driven future. We are committed to fostering a culture of innovation through accessible education and skill development in AI and related fields.

Our Mission

To empower the students, teachers, and youth of Jashpur district with cutting-edge knowledge in Artificial Intelligence, Prompting, ChatGPT, and emerging technologies, fostering a culture of innovation and digital literacy.

Our Vision

To establish Jashpur as a hub of innovation and digital excellence by nurturing a generation of technologically skilled individuals proficient in Artificial Intelligence, Prompting, ChatGPT, and emerging technologies.
