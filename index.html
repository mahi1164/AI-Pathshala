Our Story

AI Pathshala Jashpur was founded with the vision to bridge the digital divide and prepare the youth of Jashpur for a tech-driven future. We are committed to fostering a culture of innovation through accessible education and skill development in AI and related fields.

Our Mission

AI Pathshala Jashpur empowers the youth, students, and teachers of Jashpur district with knowledge in AI, ChatGPT, and emerging technologies, fostering innovation and digital literacy. The initiative bridges the rural tech divide, boosts employability, and nurtures future-ready individuals through accessible, hands-on training, building a sustainable ecosystem for skill development and national progress.

Our Values

The vision is to make Jashpur a hub of innovation and digital excellence by cultivating a tech-savvy generation skilled in AI, Prompting, ChatGPT, and emerging technologies. It aims to foster collaboration among students, educators, and professionals to address real-world challenges, drive growth, and position Jashpur as a leader in the global AI revolution.
